Our programs

Inheritance

Inheritance (Meros) is a TALIMY initiative that reconnects Uzbek youth with the intellectual legacy of scholars like Al-Khwarizmi, Al-Beruni, and Mirzo Ulugbek, transforming their discoveries into accessible educational materials distributed to 40,000+ schools nationwide. By bridging past and present, it inspires students to recognize that curiosity and innovation are part of their heritage and encourages them to carry this legacy forward.

Research Forum

The TALIMY Research Forum is a highly selective national program that brings together the most promising young researchers from schools and universities in Uzbekistan, providing two months of free mentorship and hands-on training in research fundamentals under the guidance of six professors and four supervisors. Graduates gain practical research experience, join TALIMY’s mentor network to lead sessions in their own institutions, and have their work published in the TALIMY Journal, with the inaugural Forum launching in November 2025.

Regional Network

The Regional Network is TALIMY’s nationwide initiative connecting young researchers and mentors across eight regions of Uzbekistan, hosting monthly sessions in collaboration with local universities and Youth Affairs centers to bring research culture directly to communities. By developing a standardized curriculum with university experts, the program ensures all participants receive structured training in research fundamentals, academic writing, and project methodology, advancing TALIMY’s mission to make research accessible to every youth.

About Talimy

Talimy is a pioneering national youth research initiative dedicated to building Uzbekistan's research culture from the ground up. As an official initiative under the Youth Affairs Agency of the Republic of Uzbekistan, we empower young minds to explore, question, and contribute to the nation's intellectual and scientific growth.



Talimy operates as an official national initiative, bringing together dedicated organizers, passionate researchers, and educational institutions across all regions of Uzbekistan to build a sustainable research culture for future generations.
